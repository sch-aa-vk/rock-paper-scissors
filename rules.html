Game Rules

Mode 1: Classic Rock-Paper-Scissors

The classic game mode features the traditional rock, paper, and scissors gestures:

1. Rock: Wins against scissors, loses to paper.

2. Scissors: Win against paper, lose to rock.

3. Paper: Wins against rock, loses to scissors.

Mode 2: BBT (Rock-Paper-Scissors-Lizard-Spock)

The BBT mode features additional gestures inspired by the Big Bang Theory:

1. Rock: Wins against scissors and lizard, loses to paper and Spock.

2. Scissors: Wins against paper and lizard, loses to rock and Spock.

3. Paper: Wins against rock and Spock, loses to scissors and lizard.

4. Lizard: Wins against paper and Spock, loses to rock and scissors.

5. Spock: Wins against rock and scissors, loses to paper and lizard.

Mode 3: With Well

The With Well mode introduces a special gesture, "Well," with unique interactions:

1. Rock: Wins against scissors and Well, loses to paper.

2. Scissors: Wins against paper and Well, loses to rock.

3. Paper: Wins against rock and Well, loses to scissors.

4. Well: Wins against rock, paper, and scissors.

General Rules:

1. The player makes a choice according to the rules of the selected mode.

2. After the player has made a choice, the winner is determined by the interaction of gestures.

3. If you win, you will receive points, and if not, you will lose.

4. In case of a tie, no one gets points, and the game continues.

Example of the Game Process:

1. The player chooses a gesture according to the selected mode.

2. The computer (or another player) chooses a gesture according to the selected mode.

3. The winner is determined based on the rules of the selected mode, and points are assigned accordingly.

4. Players make new choices for the next round.
